# Privacy Policy for EfficientTutor

**Last updated: December 27, 2025**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to EfficientTutor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by EfficientTutor.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use our application:
- **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your name, email address, and role (Teacher, Parent, or Student).
- **Usage Data:** We store data related to your tuition schedules, notes, logs, and financial records as entered by you or your connected teacher/student.
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about the device you use to access the app (e.g., model, operating system) to improve user experience.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain our Service (scheduling, financial tracking).
- To manage your account and authenticate your identity.
- To communicate with you regarding updates or support.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored on our backend servers (hosted on Render.com). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:
- **With Your Consent:** E.g., sharing schedule data between a linked Teacher and Student/Parent.
- **Service Providers:** We use third-party services (like Render for hosting) that process data on our behalf.
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our Service is intended for educational management. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data without your consent, please contact us.
